# Speaker Notes

## Opening

Thank them for the opportunity. Position the presentation as a practical CFO roadmap rather than a theoretical finance strategy.

Suggested line:

I have treated the brief as a scale-up finance challenge: how to give the business better control, visibility and decision support without slowing it down.

## Key message to repeat

The CFO should help LimeChain scale with control.

That means finance should become a strategic operating system: cash visibility, profitability insight, KPI rhythm, forecasting discipline and board-ready reporting.

## Tone

- Confident, not arrogant.
- Ambitious, not unrealistic.
- Practical, not academic.
- Strategic, but execution-led.

## OKRs talking point

Say yes to OKRs in principle, but not as a heavy implementation immediately.

Suggested line:

I would start with a CFO scorecard before rolling out OKRs more widely. That gives the leadership team the benefits of focus and accountability without creating unnecessary process too early.

## Closing

Suggested close:

My priority would be to build trust first, create visibility fast, improve discipline without bureaucracy, and then use finance to help LimeChain scale with confidence.
