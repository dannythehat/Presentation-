The ask / Three asks — none of them is budget
Building Confidence
I am not asking for budget.
I am asking for decision rights.
01

30 minutes
a week

The CEO joins the weekly Cash Council for the first eight weeks. Decisions get made where the cash picture is.

02

A mandate on the two disputes

Authority to settle or escalate the 90+ day disputes — including accepting a commercial haircut for speed.

03

Delivery co-owns utilisation

The definition is agreed jointly. A number only finance believes is a number nobody uses.

Give me those three, and by day 90 confidence stops being a word on a slide. It becomes a forecast you believe, a receivables book with owners, and a fixed-price model you can scale without flinching.
