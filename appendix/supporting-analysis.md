# Supporting Analysis

## Why this is not just a finance reporting problem

For a scaling business, finance needs to do more than close the books. It needs to help management understand where value is created, where cash is tied up, where margins are under pressure, and where investment decisions need better evidence.

## Suggested CFO operating model

The CFO agenda can be split into three layers:

### 1. Visibility

- Reliable management reporting.
- Cash forecast and runway view.
- Revenue, margin and project profitability.
- Budget versus actual reporting.

### 2. Discipline

- Monthly finance calendar.
- Clear ownership of inputs.
- Forecasting rhythm.
- Working capital routines.
- Board pack cadence.

### 3. Strategic control

- Pricing support.
- Margin improvement.
- Investment prioritisation.
- Funding and investor readiness.
- Scenario planning.

## Risk of being too ambitious

A full finance transformation in 12 months may sound attractive, but it can create execution risk.

A staged plan is stronger:

- First fix visibility.
- Then embed discipline.
- Then optimise and scale.

This shows ambition while staying credible.
